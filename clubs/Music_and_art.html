Music and Art

These clubs are dedicated to the Arts at Bronx Science. But what do we mean by arts? No, we don't mean just the traditional arts. We mean all kinds of art, art that is pleasing to the ear, the mind, and is edible. These clubs encompass lots of different arts, allowing students lots of leg room to express who they really are.
