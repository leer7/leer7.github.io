Game Clubs

Game Clubs here at Bronx Science seek to incoroporate many different facets within games in general. While there are clubs that indeed revolve around Video Games (e.g. our League of Legends Club), we also have clubs dedicated to other kinds of games (e.g. our Lego and Rubix Cube Clubs), and even games that people don't usually think of as games! (e.g. our Quiz Bowl Club)
